Ayal Pierce Bio

My name is Ayal Pierce. Orignially from Bergen County, NJ before college I became a volunteer Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) for my town's volunteer ambulance corps.

I am currently a Senior at Tufts University pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science with a pre-Med concentration. I am a dedicated member, and former Executive Director, of the Tufts Emergency Medical Services (TEMS). Next year, I plan on attending medical school.
